=============
Copy Entities
=============

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

Select one or more entities to copy to a different diagram. You 
can copy the entities to a newly created diagram or to a different existing 
diagram. Copying entities lets you customize diagrams to help you visualize 
the different ways your data can be represented in MongoDB. 

Steps
-----

#. :ref:`Select entities <rm-select-entities>` you want to copy. 

   From your :guilabel:`Diagram` or :guilabel:`Schema model` pane, click 
   on the entities you want to copy. 

#. Click :guilabel:`Copy entities to`, and select a diagram to copy your entities to. 

#. Click the ellipsis menu in the right pane or right-click the selected entities.

#. Click :guilabel:`Copy entities to`.

   You can select one of the following options to copy your entities to: 

   .. list-table::
      :header-rows: 1
      :widths: 40 60

      * - If you want to
        - Then
      * - Copy entities to an existing diagram
        - The copied entities are displayed with the other pre-existing entities in the diagram.
      * - Create a new diagram and copy entities to it
        - Only the copied entities are displayed.

#. Click :guilabel:`Copy entities`.
