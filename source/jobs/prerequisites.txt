=============

=============

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none

The following pages outline the details and specifics of prepping your 
databases for use with Relational Migrator.

MongoDB
-------

To migrate data into MongoDB, we recommend creating a :ref:`service
user account <rm-mongodb-service-user>` with :authrole:`readWrite` access to
your MongoDB database.
